876Match Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile details and preferences
  • Uploaded photos and verification content
  • Messages, stories, and interactions
  • Subscription and billing information
  • Device, browser, and usage data

2. How Information Is Used

876Match may use collected information to improve matchmaking, moderation, safety, communication, subscriptions, notifications, analytics, and overall platform performance.

3. Marketing Communications

Users who opt into marketing communications may receive updates, promotions, announcements, or information related to affiliated platform services.

Users may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

4. Data Sharing

876Match does not sell personal user data. Certain information may be shared with service providers necessary for platform operations such as payment processing, analytics, moderation, hosting, and security services.

5. Payment Processing

Payment information is processed by secure third-party payment providers. 876Match does not directly store full payment card details.

6. Safety & Moderation

Information may be reviewed internally for fraud prevention, moderation, identity verification, safety investigations, or policy enforcement.

7. Data Retention

876Match may retain certain information for moderation, fraud prevention, legal compliance, billing, and platform security purposes.

8. Security

876Match takes reasonable technical and administrative steps to protect user information. However, no online platform can guarantee complete security.

9. User Rights

Users may request account updates, corrections, or account removal subject to moderation, legal obligations, billing requirements, and fraud prevention reviews.

10. Contact

For privacy-related concerns or support, contact: support@876match.com
